The H. microstoma genome was sequenced by the Parasite Genomics group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, in collaboration with Pete Olson (Natural History Museum, London). The initial version of the genome was described in Tsai et al (2013). ParaSite releases 5-11 (January 2016 to October 2018) presented the November 2015 snapshot from GeneDB, corresponding to INSDC assembly HMIC002. Assembly presented here is the third consecutive version of the assembly, created by scaffolding a de novo PacBio assembly using Bionano's hybrid scaffolder (optical mapping). Further improvement was then carried out in gap5 using all the available optical map and sequence data. Manuscript in preparation.

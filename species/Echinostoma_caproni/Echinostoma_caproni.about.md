The trematode _Echinostoma caproni_, is a parasitic intestinal fluke that is widely used as a laboratory model. Members of the genus Echinostoma are cosmopolitan, however human infections mainly occurs in South East Asia and the Far East, in countries including China, Korea, Taiwan, Philippines, Malaysia, Indonesia and India.
